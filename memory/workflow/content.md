## Routing Rules

- Social media post (X, LinkedIn, Instagram, Threads, Bluesky) → `subflows/content-social-sf.md`
- Video content (script, shot list, production guide) → `subflows/content-video-sf.md`
- Written guide or article (blog, how-to, tutorial) → inline in content-wf

## Content Approval Gates

- Always propose content outline or structure before drafting the full piece
- One piece at a time — do not bundle multiple posts or scripts without separate approvals
- Never fabricate facts, statistics, or quotes

## Platform Context

- Read `references/platform-formats.md` (from `content-creator-sme`) for character limits and format rules
- Tone and length are platform-specific: X (concise), LinkedIn (narrative), YouTube (scripted)
- Activate `content-creator-sme` for audience definition and brand voice guidance
